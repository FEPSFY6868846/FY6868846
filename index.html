Abstract

The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) in medical diagnosis is having a growing impact in the healthcare industry offering faster, more accurate diagnosis’. This article will investigate to what extent we can rely on the efficiency and efficacy of AI in medical diagnosis, simultaneously addressing associated challenges such as bias’s that can be introduced alongside risks. Through a systematic review of recent studies and academic literature, this paper examines how AI is already being implemented, and the capabilities AI has in reducing diagnostic time, providing more accurate in depth analysis to assist physicians and doctors as well as improving patient outcomes through artificial intelligence’s powerful data analysis ability allowing them to optimize the operations of a healthcare facility benefiting both patients and doctors while remaining reliable, affective and trusted by everyone involved.
